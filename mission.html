Mission

At Maya Boni's Portfolio, our mission is to inspire and empower individuals from all educational backgrounds to embrace the world of coding. We believe that everyone, regardless of their prior experience or gender, has the potential to excel in technology. Our special focus is on encouraging girls to overcome perceived gender barriers in tech, fostering an inclusive environment where their passion for development can flourish. Through mentorship, resources, and a supportive community, we strive to ignite a lifelong enthusiasm for coding and innovation in every aspiring developer.
